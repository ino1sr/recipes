Ingredients:
 - 1 pound Gruyere cheese
 - 1 pound Vacherin cheese
 - 2 tablespoons cornstarch
 - 1 cup white wine
 - 1 tablespoon kirsch

Directions:
 1. Add the white wine to the fondue pot and heat.
 2. Stir in the cheeses and add the kirsch, blend with the cornstarch.
 3. Melt gently the cheese.
